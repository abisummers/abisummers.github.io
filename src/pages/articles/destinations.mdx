## Destinations

In this guide, I'm going to talk about the different train lines in Île-de-France and where you can go on each line. This is a work in progress and I will be updating it as I go along.

There are 5 different types of trains in Île-de-France:

- **Métro** - the underground trains in Paris
- **Tramway** - the trams that go around Paris
- **RER** - the suburban trains that go to across Île-de-France, passing through Paris
- **Transilien** - the suburban trains that go outwards from Paris to the rest of Île-de-France
- **TER** - the trains that go from Île-de-France to the rest of France
