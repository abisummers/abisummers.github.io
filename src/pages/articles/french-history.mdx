### Context

This really is a tool for me to use, but feel free to use it too! I'm in the process of applying for French nationality, which is honestly just a lot of waiting (which makes me anxious). I know I should be using this time to brush up on my French history, knowing that I could be asked about any of these people/events in the interview. In interviews, I often freeze so it's important that I prepare in advance to minmise this risk.

I'm not good at deciding what to work on, so I'm simplifying the decision - there will be a random person, event and fast response generated each time the page is refreshed. The goal is to spent time each day researching or revising based on the generated responses.

This is based on the _livret du citoyen_ and interview questions that I have seen. This will not cover everything, but it's a place to start and I will continue adding to the lists. It's important to note that there will always be person questions such as why you want to become French and your life in France.
